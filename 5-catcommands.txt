cat - concatenate (links things together in series)

cat command is used to read the files

cat can read more than 1 files together

$ cat apple.txt orange.txt
apple is called doctor of fruit because it helps to cure diseseases.
orange is citrus family food and act like medicine to reduce heat in the body.

2) cat file1 file2 > file3      
> it means overwrite
you can print output of one files into other and it will create newfile


$ cat apple.txt orange.txt > fruits.txt
~$ cat fruits.txt
apple is called doctor of fruit because it helps to cure diseseases.
orange is citrus family food and act like medicine to reduce heat in the body.

It also overwrite and latest result will be printed

$ cat apple.txt > fruits.txt
$ cat fruits.txt
apple is called doctor of fruit because it helps to cure diseseases.

== append means to print output under already file output==
~$ cat fruits.txt
apple is called doctor of fruit because it helps to cure diseseases.
~$ cat orange.txt >> fruits.txt
:~$ cat fruits.txt
apple is called doctor of fruit because it helps to cure diseseases.
orange is citrus family food and act like medicine to reduce heat in the body.

3) tac 
it prints in reverse ulta order like Z to A

$ cat fruits.txt
apple is called doctor of fruit because it helps to cure diseseases.
orange is citrus family food and act like medicine to reduce heat in the body.


~$ tac fruits.txt
orange is citrus family food and act like medicine to reduce heat in the body.
apple is called doctor of fruit because it helps to cure diseseases.
