рҹ”— 7. Inode Concept (VERY IMPORTANT)

Now comes the real core.

вқ“ Problem:

рҹ‘ү Linux separates:

рҹ’Ў Solution: Inode

An inode stores metadata:

рҹ‘ү BUT:
вқҢ It does NOT store filename

рҹ§  How it works:

рҹ“Ұ Example:

рҹ”Ҙ Why this matters:

System is efficient
